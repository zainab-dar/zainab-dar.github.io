About Us

Dozephin started with a simple idea: combine the calming power of ocean sounds with modern sleep technology. Our team of sleep researchers and sound designers crafted devices that use soft ocean waves, dolphin calls, and guided breathing rhythms to help users drift off naturally.

We’re passionate about helping people improve their sleep in a way that feels natural, relaxing, and sustainable. Dozephin isn’t just a product—it’s a nightly escape to your own personal beachside getaway.
