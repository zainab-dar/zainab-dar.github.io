Website Evaluation

Apple

Apple’s website is clean, modern, and user-friendly. It highlights its products effectively with high-quality visuals and minimal text.

  • Contrast: Excellent contrast. There are bold product images on simple white or black backgrounds, making their products stand out.
  • Repetition: There is a consistent use of fonts, layout, and color themes across pages. It strengthens the brand identity and familiar features throughout the pages
  • Alignment: Strong grid-based design with centered product ensures visual balance.
  • Proximity: Related information and links are grouped together, making navigation easy.

Ling’s Cars

Ling’s Cars is visually overwhelming, filled with flashing animations, clashing colors, and scattered content.

  • Contrast: Poor, there are too many bright, clashing colors that make the site visually exhausting.
  • Repetition: Inconsistent fonts, colors, and graphics create a chaotic experience.
  • Alignment: Elements are scattered without a clear structure, making the site feel disorganized.
  • Proximity: Related content is not grouped effectively, so users struggle to find information quickly.
